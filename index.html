Idea Generator

James Altucher

Develop your "idea muscle" by writing down 10 ideas every day. "If you can't come up with 10 ideas, come up with 20 ideas...You are putting too much pressure on yourself. Perfectionism is the ENEMY of the idea muscle...it's your brain trying to protect you from harm, from coming up with an idea that is embarassing and stupid and could cause you to suffer pain. The way you shut [this] off is by forcing [the brain] to come up with bad ideas."

Quoted from "Tools of Titans" by Timothy Ferris.
